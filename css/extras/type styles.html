The typography of BasicTheme is designed with a strong hierarchy in mind, yet is kept simple and maintains legibility. The base font is the very Swiss-looking typeface Helvetica Neue (Except all you PC suckers will get crappy ol' Arial) and can be changed to suit if needed.

Regular paragraphs are set at a 14px base, with a 20px baseline (line-height in CSS)

An ideal measure for 14px sized text is 420px wide, which could be considered when designing your site. The closest BasicTheme has to a 420px wide column is .six.columns, which is approximately 430px, but can be changed accordingly.

<H3> is for smaller headings.

<H4> is a bolder version of <H3>.

<H5> is for smaller, quieter headings.
